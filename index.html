About Sarah

Art is about telling a story. Artist Sarah Rose Costa has been telling stories since she could hold a crayon. At five years old she insisted that every guest in her home go home with a personally written and illustrated book. If she had the time, she still would.

These days, Sarah channels that relentless creative energy into custom illustrations, book cover designs, and portraits that capture more than just what's on the surface. Whether you're a person, a pet, or a fantastical creature yet to exist, she's ready to tell your story through art.

When she's not wielding a paintbrush or digital stylus, you might find her exploring mixed media (yes, that includes knitting and embroideryâ€”art has no boundaries), perfecting the art of caffeinated beverages, or convincing friends they definitely need a custom portrait of their cat wearing a tiny hat.
